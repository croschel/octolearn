How it works

Three steps to actually remembering

Takes less than 2 minutes to set up. Works every time you finish a study session.

01

Tell OctoLearn what you studied

Enter a subject area (AWS, Java, React) and the topics you covered today. OctoLearn can suggest topics if you're unsure where to start.

02

Take your personalized quiz

Get 10 questions — 70% multiple choice, 30% descriptive. Wrong answers get hints. 3 wrong attempts triggers a full explanation.

03

Get your report & references

Receive a score breakdown, learning resume, and curated resource list (free to paid). Save to Notion, Drive, or download as PDF.
